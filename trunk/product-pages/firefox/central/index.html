Getting Started with Firefox

A quick start guide to getting the most from your online experience

Getting Help

Firefox comes with a complete Help system. To access Firefox Help, click on the "Help" menu and select "Firefox Help".

Tutorials and other support options are also available with numerous online guides and tutorials.

Searching for Information Online

It's easier than ever to find information online. Enter a search term in the Search bar and receive immediate answers from the search engine you've chosen. You can select a new search engine from the Search bar menu at any time.
